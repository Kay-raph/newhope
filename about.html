Wellcome to our Charity

Organizational Background

New Hope Foundation (NHF) is a nonprofit organization based in South Sudan. NHF prime motto is capacity building and services delivery to the deprived and marginalized people of the society. In 2014 a group of educated, dynamic and like-minded youth working in the development sector, got together to establish an organization, for addressing the most urgent and pressing needs of the people of South Sudan. True to this spirit the organization was named New Hope Foundation (NHF) Considering their association with the development sector the group was cognizant of the plethora of problems plaguing the States. Consequently, the organization’s focus was not kept limited either in in terms of geography or sector. Whilst operating in different counties of the state, NHF has been carrying a range of interventions in the areas of education, health & hygiene, youth & women empowerment, emergency response & preparedness, governance, free legal aid, human rights, etc.

Strategy

A sustainable strategy is devised to achieve the maximum, a need based and right based approach initiated ascertains the problem with fully community involvement and mobilizing all indigenous and local resources. The program goal are materialized with a helping hand from members donor agencies, Philanthropists.

Goals & Objectives

The project is to support the vulnerable people across the country by opening up water wells, doing water sanitation and hygiene works, donating foods and tents, visiting orphanages by providing essential needs of children and also visiting youth, PWDs and women associations in order to ease their living conditions, which we aim to achieve within two years.

Objectives

Our Specific Objectives Includes:

1. To Provide quality legal assistance to juvenile victims of sexual abuse.
2. Increased household income and economic activities.

4. To Provide Education Service.

9. To increase the education of the target community for the betterment of their own subsistence.
